ANJIR

  • Moist, juicy and dry, anjir (or figs) at DryFruit Basket are a true delicacy. Anjir makes a part of every dietary recommendation, being low fat, low sodium, low cholesterol and high fiber. It also has the highest mineral content among other common fruits. Anjir helps maintain blood pressure, decreases water retention, and promotes bone health.
